    Administration is broad. This will not breakdown all the how-tos of administration. Google is your bestfriend.

    Intro#

    There are several services the company is subscrbed to for it’s IT and Online platform.

    This guide will ONLY breakdown the accounts following the knowledge base of the accounts, respectively.

    A2 Hosting#

    The company is subscribed to A2 Hosting to host our website/s.

    This is different from the service for the rpnradio.com domain which is under GoDaddy.

    Admin Panel#

    To view the admin panel, navigate and login to Admin/Customer Portal

    Knowledge Base#

    A2 Hosting has their own Knowledge Base website which can be visited here: A2 Hosting Knowledge Base for all of their how-tos.

    Microsoft 365 for Business#

    The company is subscribed to Microsoft 365 Business Basic mainly for its email services. The yearly subscription also contains other perks which you can view detailed here: Microsoft 365 for Business.

    The current company’s plan is for 42 users:

    • 2 Executives
    • 4 Managers
    • 12 OICS
    • 12 Admin & Finance Assistants
    • 6 Engineers
    • 5 Sales & Finance
    • 1 Admin

    Admin Panel#

    To view the admin panel, navigate and login to Admin Portal

    Knowledge Base#

    The how-to links below will be helpful in the occassional administration

    • Microsoft 365 Admin Center Help
    • Users & Groups
    • Email & Calenders
    • Domains
    • Subscriptions & Billing

    Streamerr and Centova Cast#

    This two dashboards are separate but come in one since they are both for the Audio Stream Servers.

    Streamerr is the main dashboard for the service subscription while Centova Cast is the control dashboard for the audio servers.

    Admin Panel#

    • Streamerr.co
    • Centova Cast

    Knowledge Base#

    • Streamerr.co Knowledge Base
    • Centova Cast User Manual

    Google#

    The company has a Google Account which also controls other Google products (e.g., Gmail, YouTube, Analytics).

    Apple#

    Just like Google, we also have an Apple account mainly for their App Developer Program which enables to access App Store Connect.

    The Developer Account is the main portal for account management while App Store Connect is the dashboard for the store/marketplace.

    Social Media#

    As of the writing, we have accounts for Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok, YouTube (Google), and Instagram.
